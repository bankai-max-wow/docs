## Models

| Model | Duration | Quality | Speed |
|-------|----------|---------|-------|
| `sora-2` | Up to 20s | Highest | Slow (3–10 min) |
| `sora-turbo` | Up to 5s | High | Fast (30–60s) |

## Features

- **Long coherent videos** up to 20 seconds
- **Multiple resolutions**: 480p, 720p, 1080p
- **Aspect ratios**: 16:9, 9:16, 1:1
- Understands physics and real-world dynamics

## Example

```python
response = client.post("/videos/generations", body={
    "model": "sora-turbo",
    "prompt": "Aerial drone view of a Japanese castle surrounded by cherry blossoms, golden hour",
    "duration": 5,
    "aspect_ratio": "16:9"
})

print(response["id"])  # Poll this ID for completion
```

## Tips

- Sora excels at cinematic descriptions with lighting and camera movement cues
- Use `sora-turbo` for rapid iteration; `sora-2` for final production
- Generation takes 3–10 minutes for `sora-2`; 30–90 seconds for `sora-turbo`
