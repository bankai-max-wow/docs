## Models

| Model | Duration | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| `veo-3` | Up to 10s | Audio generation included |
| `veo-3.1` | Up to 10s | Improved motion coherence |

## Unique Feature: Native Audio

Veo 3 generates **synchronized audio** with your video — background sounds, music, and ambient noise.

## Example

```python
response = client.post("/videos/generations", body={
    "model": "veo-3",
    "prompt": "A thunderstorm over Tokyo at night, rain on neon signs, thunder in the distance",
    "duration": 8,
    "aspect_ratio": "16:9"
})
```

<Note>
  Veo 3 produces videos with audio by default. The audio is generated to match the visual content.
</Note>
